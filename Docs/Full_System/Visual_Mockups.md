# Visual Mockups

As the Business Analyst / UX Designer, I have generated visual mockups for three key interfaces of the AI Digital Content Platform. These mockups represent the premium, modern design aesthetic required for the project.

## 1. Creator App Dashboard (Mobile)
This mockup represents the independent Creator (Cộng Đồng) landing page. It focuses on the daily AI topic suggestions and immediate tracking of earnings to drive engagement.

![Creator Dashboard Mockup](/Users/lyhuynh/.gemini/antigravity/brain/71f0aa75-e792-42bb-8b8c-7c90c146ec28/creator_dashboard_mockup.jpeg)

**Key Elements:**
- Horizontal carousel for AI Topic Ideas.
- Clear "Create Video" CTA.
- Revenue tracking chart at the bottom.
